Situated at the heart of Amman, The House is within walking distance from the hustle and bustle of the city, from shopping streets to government centers. And for those who are looking for a medical retreat, The House is only a short drive away from major hospitals and medical centers.

WELCOME TO THE FAMILY

Our Loyalty Program provides members with an elevated experience from the moment they enter The House until the moment they check out. Members enjoy special rates, offers, and access to exclusive benefits across all facilities.

Benefits

  • 10% discount on all suites for the first time only (upon joining the family)

  • 10% discount at any of Um Khalil Lebanese Restaurant’s branches

  • Guaranteed 24-hour room availability prior to arrival

  • Early check-in at 11 am

  • Late check-out till 3 pm

  • Choice of local newspaper

Policies

  • 10 JD = 1 Point

  • Points are converted to Jordanian Dinars

  • Points can be used in F&B bills and room bills

  • Booking should be made by phone, email, our official website, or directly at the front desk

  • Points collected through online booking cannot be redeemed in the same booking session.

  • Points are recorded in the members’ personal account once their check-out process is complete.
